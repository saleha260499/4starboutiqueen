## Social Impact Commitment

4 Star Boutiqueen is committed to creating a positive social impact through its business.

At the end of each month, 5% of the company's net profit is allocated to charitable initiatives.

### Phase 1
- Purchase and distribute meals to people in need.

### Future Vision
As the business grows, the company aims to:
- Support education for underprivileged Muslim communities.
- Sponsor school supplies, tuition, and educational programs.
- Expand charitable initiatives while maintaining business sustainability.

The company's mission is to build a profitable business that creates lasting social impact.

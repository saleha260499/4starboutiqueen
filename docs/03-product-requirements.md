# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name
4 Star Boutiqueen

## Product Goal

Build a modern, scalable, and user-friendly e-commerce platform for modest fashion that enables customers to discover, purchase, and manage clothing products online while helping the business automate operations and grow internationally.

---

# Functional Requirements (MVP)

1. User Registration & Login
2. Browse Products by Category
3. Search Products by Name
4. View Product Details
5. Add to Cart
6. Add to Wishlist
7. Secure Checkout
8. View Order History
9. Admin Product Management
10. Trending / Best Seller Section
